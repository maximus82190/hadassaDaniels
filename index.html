Film Composer

My name is Hadassa Daniels, and I am an Israel-based composer with experience in film scoring, arrangement, and orchestration. Having developed a passion for film and music as a young child, I went on to study for two years at the Rimon School of Music, received a BMus in multidisciplinary composition from the Jerusalem Academy of Music and Dance, and am currently completing my Masterâ€™s degree in composition for media and film. I have been fortunate to participate in international collaborations with schools in the United States to write scores for student films, and have composed for a broad range of genres, including documentary, drama, horror, and action. There is great power in storytelling, and in the ability of music to elegantly complement and move a plot forward. It is important for me to understand a film in depth when writing the score, in order to capture every nuanced expression or emotion, every meaningful or suspenseful moment. When done right, music can take an audience from being a spectator to becoming a participant in the unfolding of a story. It is my wish to create music that will do the utmost justice to the film it serves.
